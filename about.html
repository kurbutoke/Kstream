About Kstream

Kstream is a modern, fast streaming interface built for people who want to discover great movies and TV shows without friction.

Instant discovery

Search, browse trending, and explore categories with speed-first UI and responsive design.

Flexible playback

Multiple streaming providers help you keep watching when one source is busy or down.

Local-first library

Your favorites and watch history are stored locally in your browser for better privacy.

Kstream does not host any files. It links to media hosted by third‑party services.
