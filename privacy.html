Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This page explains how Kstream operates.

Local data only

Favorites and watch history are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. You can clear them anytime.

Third‑party players

Kstream embeds players from external streaming providers. Those providers may set their own cookies.

No user accounts

Kstream does not require sign‑up or collect personal information.

Kstream is a streaming discovery experience.

We do not store any files on our server.
